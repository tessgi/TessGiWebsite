TESS mission

All TESS Cycle 1 data available at MAST
01 Sep 2019

TESS has officially completed its survey of the southern sky, observing thirteen sectors in the southern ecliptic hemisphere between July 2018 and July 2019. All of the Cycle 1 data are now publicly available at MAST. Details of the available data can be found at the MAST site.
