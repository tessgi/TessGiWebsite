TESS Sector 1 and 2 data to be available on December 6

TESS data products from Sectors 1 and 2 will begin to go public starting Thursday December 6 at 9:00 AM US EST. These data will be available at the MAST archive. Both Full Frame Images and 2-minute cadence postage stamps will be included in the release.
