Approved Guest Investigator programs from the primary mission

This page details the Guest Investigator programs that were executed by TESS during its primary mission (July 2018-July 2020).

The target lists are posted on this page as soon as the team has prepared the final target list for upload to the spacecraft, which happens shortly before the start of a sector.

You can click on the title of each program to read a summary of the research objectives.

For GI programs executed as part of the first extension of the mission (July 2020-September 2022) please see our first extension proposals page .

For GI programs that will be executed as part of the second extension of the mission (September 2022-September 2025) please see our second extension proposals page .

Cycle 2

Cycle 2 contains all targets proposed through the Guest Investigator program for the second year of the TESS mission. All programs are to observe sources in the Northern Ecliptic hemisphere.
